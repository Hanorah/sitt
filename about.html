SKILLS INCOME AND TECHNOLOGY
EMPOWERING THE NEXT GENERATION

What is SIT?

The Skills, Income, and Technology (SIT) program is a 4-week immersive course designed for youths aged 13-19 to develop essential skills in creativity, technology, and career-building. Your child will learn from industry experts, gain hands-on experience, and showcase their achievements in a final exhibition.

Why Should Your Child Register?

The SIT program offers more than just education; it's a transformative experience that prepares your child for the future. By joining, they will:
- Develop practical skills in high-demand fields like web development, content creation, and digital media.
- Learn directly from industry experts, receiving personalized mentorship.
- Build confidence through real-world projects.
- Connect with peers and mentors, forming valuable networks for their future career.
With technology driving tomorrow, the skills your child gains today will set them apart.

What Are the Requirements for SIT Admission?

To qualify for SIT, applicants must:
- Be aged 13-19 years.
- Have a keen interest in technology, creativity, and innovation.
- Submit a brief statement explaining their motivation for joining the program.
These requirements ensure a vibrant and motivated group of participants.
